  1. Carbon dioxide capture and utilization

    2. We use a multi-scale approach to find suitable adsorbent materials to capture CO2 from coal-fired power plants. Using electronic structure calculations, the group investigates catalysts which can transform CO2 into useful chemicals, such as cyclic carbonate.
