  2. Carbon Capture and Utilization

    3. We use a multi-scale approach to find suitable technology to capture CO2 from coal-fired power plants. The group also collaborates with experimentalists to investigate chemical transformation of CO2 into useful chemicals, such as cyclic carbonate.
